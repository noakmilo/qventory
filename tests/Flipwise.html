Automatic ingestion of sales data

When something sells, Flipwise knows immediately, and ingests all sales data automatically—including your cost to ship—and reports on your net return, ROI, net profit margin, and more.

Source new inventory wisely

Flipwise makes it easy to understand the performance of your sourcing locations so you can focus your efforts where ROIs are highest.

Automatic ending and relisting

Flipwise can automatically end and relist your eBay listings while keeping their full history intact. Whether an item has been ended and relisted, cancelled and relisted, or returned and relisted—even across multiple listing IDs—Flipwise tracks and retains those connections. It retroactively knows the full history of your entire inventory, both active and sold, going back up to two years.

Set listing goals and stick to them

Listing new inventory regularly is the single most important thing you can do to keep the eBay algorithm happy. Flipwise makes it easy to stay on track.
