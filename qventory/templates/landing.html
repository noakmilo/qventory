{# ===== FAQ ===== #}

Frequently Asked Questions

How does eBay sync work?

Connect your eBay account via OAuth (secure, no password sharing). Qventory imports your active listings, sales, and order data automatically. Updates sync in real-time.

What is auto-relist and how does it help?

Auto-relist refreshes stale eBay listings to boost visibility. Qventory supports scheduled Update & Relist with price drops, floor prices, bulk controls, and safety rules for sold or active-order items.

What is Thrift Radar?

Thrift Radar is a sourcing tool for resellers. Search by ZIP code, city, or optional location to find thrift stores, flea markets, and outlets, then save searches and routes. It is currently focused on the U.S.

Can Qventory create new eBay listings?

Yes. The eBay Listing Wizard helps create listings with AI-assisted titles, descriptions, item specifics, image editing, SKU generation, category search, listing format, and draft management.

What is the difference between Update & Relist and Price Update?

Update & Relist creates a refreshed relisted item on a schedule. Price Update keeps the same live listing and gradually lowers the price like a Dutch Auction until it reaches your floor price.

Does it track sales and profit?

Yes! All sales are tracked with marketplace fees, shipping costs, and calculated net profit. Filter by date, marketplace, or status. Export sales data anytime.

Can I track fulfillment and shipping?

Absolutely. Monitor all orders from payment to delivery. Track shipping carriers, tracking numbers, and delivery status in one unified dashboard.

Is AI pricing accurate?

Our AI analyzes eBay market trends to provide data-driven estimates. Always verify with the "Search sold listings" link provided in reports.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes! Install as a PWA (Progressive Web App) on iOS or Android. Full QR scanning, dark mode, and optimized for phones.

Which marketplaces are supported?

eBay full integration (sync, sales, fulfillment). Track links for Amazon, Mercari, Vinted, Poshmark, Depop. Profit calculator supports eBay & Depop fees.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your inventory is yours. We don't sell data. eBay connection uses secure OAuth. Export all data anytime via CSV.
